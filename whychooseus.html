WHY YOU SHOULD SELECT US

There are many specific reasons as to why you should choose Sinha Homes to build your house.

OUR PRICE

Our prices are very competitive. We may not be the cheapest, but we offer attractive pricing for high quality houses in the market.

OUR SERVICE

Outstanding customer service, high quality materials, fair business practices and extraordinary workmanship are part of our commitment to deliver excellence.

OUR DESIGN

Our experienced architects design houses that bring mental happiness, family harmony and good health.

OUR QUALITY

We use quality raw materials supplied by registered and well‑renowned suppliers, inspecting all materials closely for quality and standards.

PROBLEMS YOU FACE WHEN BUILDING A HOUSE

  • If you do not have guidance you may spend unnecessary money beyond your budget.
  • You might be deceived by people involved in the construction.
  • You may not know where to find quality materials.
  • The finishing of your house may not meet your dream.

OUR INSPECTION

Our qualified engineers visit weekly to inspect the quality of the house. They personally check the land and construction materials very closely.

OUR SPECIALTIES

The relationship built between you and us will last with no limitations. Customer satisfaction is our top priority—we carefully build your dream home to your satisfaction.

WHAT IS OUR SPECIALITY

We minimize unnecessary costs through proper guidance. You can be assured that the raw materials used in our constructions meet high standards.

Having a well‑developed home construction specification, a detailed budget and good house plans makes construction financing much easier and faster.
